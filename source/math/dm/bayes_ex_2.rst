Consider a binary classification problem `\theta \in \{0, 1\}`:

.. math::
   
   \theta =
   \begin{cases}
     0, & \text{Tail}
     \\
     1, & \text{Head}
   \end{cases}

The prior probability of `\theta`:

.. math::

   p_{\Theta}(\theta) =
   \begin{cases}
     0.69, & \theta = 0
     \\
     0.31, & \theta = 1
   \end{cases}
